About Aquila

Aquila, a guild in World of Warcraft based on the Hellscream realm in Europe was created in April 2007. We formed after demerging from a much larger older guild, Divine Protectors which dated back to the realms opening in spring 2005. Basically we were a bunch of in-game (and some real life) friends who wanted to concentrate more on 25 man raiding.

While we had no intention of climbing the realm progression ranks we steadily did so. We finished out "The Burning Crusade" as realm No. 2. A place we have maintained through Wrath of the Lich King and as a 10 man raid group in Cataclysm. Several years may have passed but we still count a significant number of original members amongst our ranks.
